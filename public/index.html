What we do at Newton and Emberly

Newton and Emberly Limited is a multitasking company with special interests and expertise in Trade, Marine Services, Exportation, Farms, Logistics, Technology, and Real Estate. The company was conceptualized and started operations in 2015 and recently got registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission as a Limited Liability company under the Company and Allied Matters Act. Each business branch of Newton and Emberly Limited provides excellent services to the admirations and commendations of customers while putting into action the mission, vision and philosophy of the company.

Health and Safety

Health and Safety

Employee Health and Safety is fundamental to the operating philosophy of Newton and Emberly limited. We ensure that no business operation will be pursued at the detriment of safety, ensuring compliance to rules and regulations guiding the industry via regular and routine safety drills and quality management procedures. We ensure a working environment free from substance abuse with high standards of occupational health tests. This is achieved through regular and stringent alcohol and drug tests. We provide personnel with proper equipment and training in accordance with their duties. Strictly adhere to tested policies that enhance safety like Smoking Policy, Clear Deck Policy, Drug and Alcohol Policy. Our continued objectives are to: Control Risk and Have ZERO accidents or cases of work- related ill health. It is the policy of Newton and Emberly Limited to provide the highest practical standards of health, safety and environment at all time. We therefore ensure that safe and healthy working conditions are provided for all employees and to ensure that work performed by the company has no adverse effect on the health and safety of third parties.
